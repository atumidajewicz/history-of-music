Learning About Jazz Music

What is a jazz music?

Jazz was born in New Orleans about 100 years ago. It's roots can be found in the musical traditions of Africa and Europe. The word "jazz" probably came from a slang word "jasm", which originally meant energy, vitality, spirit, pep. The slang word "jasm" can be traced back as far as 1860 in the English language. Jazz was born into a new world of emancipation and freedom, stimulating a spirit of experimentation and expression. Some of the composers in this genre would include John Coltrane, Miles Davis, and Louis Armstrong.
