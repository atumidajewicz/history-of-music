Learning About The Blues

What is blues music?

The history of the blues is poorly documented. The blues developed in the southern United States after the American Civil War (1861-1865). The blues evolved from the oral tradition of African American work songs and spirituals, which featured the call-and-response patterns that are still prominent in today's blues. Some of the composers from this genre would include Robert Johnson, John lee Hooker and Son House.
